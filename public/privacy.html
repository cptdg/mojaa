Mojaa Privacy Notice

This policy explains how Mojaa (“we”, “us”) handles personal data for the global brands we buy from and the African customers we serve through our ecommerce storefronts.

This landing page does not process checkouts or payments. It only redirects you to the appropriate ecommerce site (ma.mojaa.com, ng.mojaa.com, eg.mojaa.com) where transactions take place and where the full commerce experience is hosted.

1. What this page does

This Mojaa portal is a simple entry point that helps you choose which regional ecommerce site to visit. No customer accounts, orders, or payments are processed on this page; those activities happen only on the individual storefronts operated in each country.

2. Data we collect on this portal

When you browse this page, we collect a limited amount of technical information so we can understand how the portal is used and keep it secure.

  • Basic device and usage data (such as browser type, pages viewed, approximate location)
  • Standard log data generated by our hosting and security providers
  • Analytics information (page views and link clicks) captured via Google Analytics

3. Local ecommerce sites

When you click through to a country storefront (for example, ma.mojaa.com, ng.mojaa.com or eg.mojaa.com), your shopping experience – including checkout, delivery, and customer support – is provided by the local operating company for that market. The privacy policy and terms published on each storefront govern how your personal data is processed for those services.
