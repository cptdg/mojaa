Mojaa Privacy Notice

This policy explains how Mojaa (“we”, “us”) handles personal data for the global brands we buy from and the African customers we serve through our ecommerce storefronts.

This landing page does not process checkouts or payments. It only redirects you to the appropriate ecommerce site (ma.mojaa.com, ng.mojaa.com, eg.mojaa.com) where transactions take place and where the full commerce experience is hosted.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the data that helps us source inventory from partner brands, manage owned logistics, and deliver customer support.

  • Identity & contact details provided during onboarding
  • Operational data (purchase orders, shipments, fulfillment notes) managed within our fulfillment network
  • Product media and catalog information supplied for Mojaa-owned listings
  • Customer order details, delivery addresses, and support history collected on our ecommerce sites (not on this landing page)
  • Analytics data (page views, link clicks) captured via Google Analytics when you browse this portal

2. How We Use Data

Primary uses include:

  • Procurement from partner brands and distribution of Mojaa-owned products
  • Fraud prevention and operational safety across North/West Africa
  • Legal compliance with Moroccan and African trade regulations

3. Data Sharing & Transfers

We only share data with vetted fulfillment partners, customs brokers, cloud and analytics providers, and shipping partners that support Mojaa services.

4. Data Retention

Operational records are retained for as long as accounts stay active and for the minimum periods required by Moroccan law.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, deletion, or export of your personal data using the contact details below.
